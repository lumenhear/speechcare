Our Services

Comprehensive audiology and speech therapy solutions

Audiology Services

Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA)

Comprehensive evaluation of hearing sensitivity using air and bone conduction testing.

Impedance Audiometry

Tests middle ear function and eardrum movement to assess hearing health.

Otoacoustic Emissions (OAE)

Newborn and pediatric hearing screening for early detection of hearing issues.

BERA/ABR Testing

Brainstem Evoked Response Audiometry for auditory nerve and brainstem function assessment.

Bone Conduction ABR

Specialized hearing test using skull bone vibration for accurate diagnosis.

VRA/Conditioned Testing

Visual Reinforcement Audiometry and behavioral tests for young children.

Hearing Aid Services

Trial, fitting, real ear measurements, and servicing for all hearing aid brands.

Speech & Language Therapy

Articulation Disorders

Treatment for speech sound production difficulties and pronunciation issues.

Delayed Speech & Language

Comprehensive therapy for children with speech and language development delays.

Fluency/Stuttering

Specialized treatment for stuttering and other fluency disorders.

Voice Disorders

Treatment for voice quality, pitch, and volume issues.

Autism Communication

Specialized communication therapy for individuals with autism spectrum disorders.

Post-Stroke Aphasia

Rehabilitation therapy for language difficulties following stroke or brain injury.
