Frequently Asked Questions

We provide services for patients of all ages, from newborns to seniors. Our pediatric services are specially designed for children, while we also have extensive experience working with adults and elderly patients.

Common signs you may need a hearing test include: difficulty understanding conversations, frequently asking people to repeat themselves, turning up the TV volume too high, trouble hearing in noisy environments, or ringing in your ears (tinnitus).

The duration varies depending on the individual and the specific speech or language disorder. Some patients show improvement within weeks, while others may require several months of therapy. We'll provide a personalized treatment plan after your initial evaluation.
